# Course Chat

The Course Chat is your general-purpose assistant for an entire course. Whether you have a question about a lecture concept, need a topic explained differently, or want to explore related ideas, this is the place to start.

## How to Access It

Open the **course sidebar** in Artemis and click on the Course Chat entry. The chat opens as a panel where you can start typing questions right away.

## What Iris Knows

In Course Chat, Iris has access to:

- **Lecture slides and transcripts** — all lectures that have been uploaded to the course
- **Course FAQs** — frequently asked questions curated by your instructors
- **General course topics** — concepts covered in the course syllabus and materials

This means you can ask about anything covered in the course without having to specify which lecture or exercise you mean — Iris searches across all available materials.

## Citations

When Iris draws on specific course content, you will see small **numbered citation markers** in the response (for example, [1], [2]). Hover over a citation to see which lecture slide, video segment, or FAQ entry it came from. For more details on how citations work, see [How Iris Helps You Learn](./how-iris-helps#citations).

## Example Use Cases

- "What is the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning?" — Iris explains the concept using your course's lecture content.
- "Can you summarize the key points from the lecture on recursion?" — Iris retrieves relevant slides and provides a summary.
- "I don't understand the observer pattern. Can you explain it with a simple example?" — Iris explains the concept and may cite the specific lecture that covered it.
- "What topics were covered in last week's lecture?" — Iris can search across all available lecture materials to find the answer.

:::tip
Course Chat is best for **conceptual questions** about the course material. If you need help with a specific exercise you are working on, use the [Exercise Chat](./exercise-chat) instead — it can see your code and build results.
:::

## Chat History

Course Chat keeps a history of your past conversations, organized by date in the sidebar. You can:

- **Resume a previous conversation** by clicking on it in the sidebar.
- **Start a fresh conversation** by clicking the pen icon at the top of the chat.

Starting a new chat is useful when you want to change topics, since Iris uses the conversation history to understand context.
